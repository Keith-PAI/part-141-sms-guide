# Part 141 SMS & QMS Readiness Guide

A single-page informational guide helping Part 141 certificated flight schools understand the National Flight Training Alliance's proposed SMS and QMS requirements.

## Background

On March 31, 2026, the FAA-backed National Flight Training Alliance released a 471-page report proposing sweeping changes to Part 141 flight training regulations, including mandatory Safety Management Systems (SMS) and Quality Management Systems (QMS) for all certificated flight schools. The FAA comment period closes April 10, 2026.

## What's in the Guide

- Overview of the five major NFTA proposals (CMO, SMS, QMS, simulation credit, examining authority)
- Practical breakdown of what SMS means for flight schools, including the four pillars
- Self-assessment checklist to gauge readiness
- Information about PAI Consulting's SMS and QMS services
